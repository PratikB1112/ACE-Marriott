Overview of ACE Marriott

Open here since 2010

Since 2010, when it first opened its doors, ACE Marriott Pune has become an iconic landmark in Pune city and has come to epitomize a seductive blend of luxury and innovative guest experiences that delight at every step.

Wide chain of hotels all over India

With the debut of ACE Marriott Mumbai Sahar, the ACE Marriott brand now has seven hotels in India. Other locations include Bangalore, Chandigarh, Mumbai, New Delhi, Pune and Mussoorie.

Plenty of Awards

ACE Marriott Pune, one of India’s leading luxury hotels, has been awarded with the India Nightlife Convention & Awards in multiple categories for their rooftop restaurant & lounge; Paasha. Paasha received the INCA Bar For wines 2019, INCA Bar in a Hotel 2019, INCA Lounge of the year, INCA Open Air Bar of the year and City Level Winner for the 4th Edition of India Nightlife Convention & Awards – Pune.

More Details

This luxery hotel is a 13-minute walk from Chaturshringi Temple and 13 km from Aga Khan Palace. The contemporary rooms have minibars and marble bathrooms, plus flat-screens and Wi-Fi (fee). Upgraded rooms add access to a private lounge serving free breakfast, drinks and snacks. Suites add kitchenettes and balconies. Turndown service is offered, as is room service. Parking is free. The hotel has 5 restaurants, including a rooftop eatery, a deli, and an Italian restaurant serving wood-fired pizzas, along with a night club and a refined bar. There's also a spa, an outdoor pool and a gym, plus a kids' play area, a business centre and 12 event rooms.
